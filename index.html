Built for how you actually spend

Most expense trackers only track expenses. Kippit tracks the full picture — what you spent, what you owe, and what's coming.

⚡

Log in under 3 taps

Calculator-style numpad. Tap a favorite chip and the amount, category, and payment method fill automatically.

💳

Repayment tracking

Credit card and mortgage repayments tracked separately — they never inflate your spending totals.

🔄

Transfer tracking

Loading Octopus from Visa? Record it as a transfer — it moves between payment methods without counting as spending.

📅

Scheduled entries

Set up recurring expenses (bus pass, school fees) and repayments (mortgage due date). They appear as upcoming in your ledger.

📊

Monthly estimate

Actuals + scheduled remaining + projected daily spend. See your real end-of-month cash outflow before the month ends.

🔔

Reminders

Get notified before scheduled payments are due. Daily nudge to log your expenses. Budget alerts when you're close.

☁️

iCloud sync

Your data syncs privately across all your devices through your own iCloud account. No account needed.

📤

Export & import

Export as JSON or CSV. Import back from any previous export. Schedule definitions and upcoming instances included.

📱

Lock Screen widget

Today's spend and your next due payment — always visible without opening the app. Siri Shortcuts for hands-free logging.

The Add screen

One screen. Three entry types.

Switch between Expense, Repayment, and Transfer with a single tap. The button colour tells you exactly which mode you're in.

Expenses are what you spend day to day — coffee, groceries, transit. They count toward your spending totals and show in the category breakdown.

  • Category + payment method selector
  • Favorites for one-tap common entries
  • Backdate to any past date
  • Red Add button — always clear which mode you're in

Repayments are when you pay off your credit card bill or mortgage instalment. They count toward total cash out but never inflate your spending totals.

  • Account selector (Visa, Amex, Mortgage)
  • Amber Add button — visually distinct
  • Appears in ledger with amber accent
  • Shown separately on Dashboard
Simple by design

Up and running in minutes

No account, no setup, no bank connections. Just open the app and start logging.

01

Install free

Download from the App Store. No account required. Default categories and payment methods pre-seeded so you can log immediately.

02

Log daily

Tap the Add tab. Type or use a favorite. Hit Add. Done in under 3 taps. Backdate anything you forgot.

03

Set up schedules

Add your mortgage, credit card due dates, bus pass, school fees. Kippit shows them as upcoming and reminds you before they're due.

04

Know your outflow

The Dashboard shows spending, repayments, and a month-end projection. Total cash out — the real number — always visible.

Privacy first

Your finances stay on your device

Kippit stores everything locally using Apple's SwiftData. Optional iCloud sync uses your own private iCloud container — the developer never sees your data.
